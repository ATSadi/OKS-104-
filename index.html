Sports at KUET

OKS plans and runs sporting events for students at Khulna University of Engineering & Technology.

About OKS

The Organisation of Kuet Sports (OKS) is one of the active clubs at KUET. We coordinate outdoor and indoor competitions and help host major national-level events on campus.

What we organise

Outdoor

  • Rag football tournament
  • Inter-department football
  • Cricket & futsal

Indoor

OKS indoor tournament includes:

  • Chess, carrom, table tennis
  • Cards, UNO, and more

National events

OKS also supports organising national-level sports events at KUET, bringing teams and audiences from across the country.
